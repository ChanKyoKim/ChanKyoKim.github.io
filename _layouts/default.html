Chankyo Kim

I am a student at Dept. of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering and Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University.

My research focuses on the field of dynamics and control of safe robotic system. Specifically, I am interested in robotic vision and knowledge transfer for enabling collaborative robot to adapt fast to unseen surroundings and nonlinear optimization, integration of sensing and robot perception for safe operation, and combining data-driven methods with control theory for solving dyanmic control assignments in unexpected environments maintaing safety and efficiency.
