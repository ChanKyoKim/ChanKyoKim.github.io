Chankyo Kim

Hi! My name is Chankyo (Peter) Kim. I am a Master's student at the University of Michigan.

I am working on policy optimization in enforcement learning for dynamic robot systems. Currently, I am working with Prof. Ram Vasudevan on accurate contact simulation for bipedal robot control. Before joining the University of Michigan, I worked as a SLAM Researcher in Bear Robotics! I received a B.S.c degree at the Dept. of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Seoul National University./p>

I would like to contribute to the academy and society with researches on efficient human-robot collaboration and reliable autonomous systems.
