Chankyo Kim

I’m Master's Student at UMICH CURLY, advised by Prof. Maani Ghaffari. My current research interest lies in Lie Group, Representation Learning, and Geometries.

I was an AI research engineer at Intellicon Metalab Inc. At Metalab, I developed a voice generation models for apply in Lip-Synced Video Synthesis for VR Simulator. I also conducted research on representation learning for temporal data (e.g., voice data of announcer).

I received my B.S. degree in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering from Seoul National University in 2022.
